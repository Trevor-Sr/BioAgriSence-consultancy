About BioAgriSence

We provide integrated scientific and agricultural consulting services, specializing in transforming research into sustainable outcomes. Our expertise encompasses Biosensing and Molecular Diagnostics for rapid pathogen and quality detection, Gene Editing (CRISPR) for advanced crop trait development, and robust **Seed Systems optimization. We complement these technical offerings with specialized Potato Agronomy consulting and comprehensive Training and Capacity Building programs to ensure the successful adoption and field-level execution of modern AgTech strategies.
